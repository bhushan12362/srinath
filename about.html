About Srinath Agritech Nursery

Srinath Agritech is a well-established plant nursery in India, offering a wide range of plants, including flowering, fruit-bearing, medicinal, and decorative plants. As a trusted wholesale plant nursery in India, we supply healthy and well-nurtured plants to landscapers, garden centers, and businesses nationwide. Our collection also features landscaping plants in India, perfect for quick and easy plantation in homes, offices, and outdoor spaces.

With years of expertise and a commitment to quality, we ensure that every plant is grown with care. Choose Shailesh Nursery for premium plants and expert guidance to enhance your green spaces.

We commit to give in our level best to make your gardening and landscaping experience a remarkable one. We encourage our customers to share their feedback at all stages so that we can serve you with better products.
